Apps2Jobs improves the job search experience with a gamified app, great advice and a fun community. Join and make job searching fun and easy!

Features:

  • Apps2Jobs tracks your job applicatins and keeps you informed and organized during your search. On all your devices.
  • App2Jobs suggests tips and tricks to improve your apps.
  • Apps2Jobs gamifies your search.
  • Apps2Jobs is a resource made by job searchers for job searchers. No companies allowed.
  • Apps2Jobs is discreet. Your information is yours and we keep it private. We only aggregate anonymous usage statistics to show you how the community is doing.
  • Apps2Jobs learns from the community. What are you going to teach us today?
